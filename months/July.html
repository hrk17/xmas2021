July

You arrived in Dallas!!! I finally got to show you my hometown. We went to White Rock Lake, visited Gail (and got baptized in the rain along the way), had coffee, went to the drive-in, and generally had a cozy and fun home existence together. It was SOOOO good to see you after so long apart, and I loved every moment of having you here. I received many fun photos from your mom of y'all out shopping, and I love all of them. The chair one I've included on this page is a particular fave. I'm really proud of you for how you've handled this huge leap into a new phase of life in a new place <3
