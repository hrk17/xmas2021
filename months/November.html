November

Alumni weekend happened! It was an insanely packed weekend, and I'm so glad you were there as my Home Base to return to when it was socially overhwhelming. I loved seeing you interact with the alums as a group, and it was great getting to meet Eng Seng and Jake and Nik and feel accepted/close to them so quickly because of you being so adored by them, and me getting to reap the rewards by extension :p This weekend we had a bigger talk about emotional needs and social styles and such, and so I remember trying to be more conscious of encouraging what you need, like you heading out to sing along at karaoke. I think this month we both were working towards being more aware of respective needs re: social time with others, alone time, and reassurance/affection interspersed with alone/social time, and I think that made this a happier month overall.

You also came down for initiation - I genuinely do not know what we/I would have done without you. You were a ridiculously massive help, and made a stressful night survivable. It was fun setting up the tent in the woods with you, and having you there for support the whole time. Thank you!
